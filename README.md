# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis using Python

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce transaction data to uncover insights about sales performance, customer behavior, and business growth opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall revenue performance
- Identify sales trends over time
- Find top-performing products and countries
- Perform customer segmentation using RFM analysis
- Generate actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Data Cleaning
- Removed missing Customer IDs
- Filtered out negative values (returns)

### 2. Feature Engineering
- Created Revenue column
- Extracted Month from InvoiceDate

### 3. KPI Analysis
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value

### 4. Exploratory Data Analysis
- Monthly revenue trends
- Sales by country
- Product performance

### 5. Customer Segmentation (RFM)
- Segmented customers into:
  - Best Customers
  - Loyal Customers
  - At Risk Customers
  - Average Customers

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Revenue shows strong seasonal trends with peaks in Q4
- The UK is the dominant revenue-generating market
- A small number of products drive most sales
- High-value customers contribute significantly to revenue
- Many customers are at risk, indicating retention challenges

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations
- Focus on customer retention strategies
- Re-engage inactive customers
- Expand into new markets beyond the UK
- Optimize product and inventory strategy
- Leverage seasonal demand patterns

---

## 📂 Dataset
E-commerce transactional dataset containing customer purchases, product details, and sales information.
